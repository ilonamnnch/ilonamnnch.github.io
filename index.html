About

I am a research assistant at KIT and an MSc student at the University of Strasbourg, focusing on analog and mixed-signal integrated circuits for advanced instrumentation.

My work is centered on mixed-signal ASIC architectures, time-domain data conversion, and hardware-oriented workflows that connect system-level intent to silicon-ready implementation (schematics, verification, and layout-aware design).

Research

Time-Based ADC (Tb-ADC) ASIC

Design and documentation of a time-domain data conversion ASIC workflow, focusing on robust mixed-signal architecture, block-level validation, and implementation-ready design choices.

Documentation: tbADC_doc

AstroPix v5 — PCB Development

PCB development for AstroPix v5, supporting mixed-signal detector readout and system-level integration. Focus on clean bring-up, debug-friendly design, and measurement-ready interfaces.

  • Board architecture & interface planning
  • Bring-up strategy (test points, measurement access, debug flow)
  • Integration with ASIC readout chain

Tools

Cadence Virtuoso / Spectre • DRC/LVS flow • Mixed-signal verification • PCB workflows
